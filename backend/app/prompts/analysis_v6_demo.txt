- Front: front 3/4 or registration/plate area.
- Tag: registration sticker, fleet asset label, or door jamb sticker.
- VEHICLE DAMAGE CALIBRATION (critical — do NOT apply IT-asset standards to vehicles):

  • Single panel dent or bumper crack visible at normal distance: MINOR/MODERATE cosmetic; condition_adjustment_pct max -8%. India's Cars24/Team-BHP data confirms single-panel cosmetic damage reduces value by only 3–8%.
  • Multiple panel damage across 3+ panels: MODERATE, condition_adjustment_pct -8% to -15%.
  • Windscreen crack: MODERATE, affects_function=true only if driver visibility is directly impaired.
  • Underbody rust (coastal_humid site): MODERATE if surface-only; SEVERE only if structural frame perforation confirmed.
  • Frame/chassis deformation, airbag deployment, engine failure, transmission failure: SEVERE, condition_adjustment_pct -20% to -35%.
- condition_adjustment_pct for vehicles: max -10% for cosmetic-only damage; -10% to -25% for combined cosmetic+functional issues; NEVER exceed -35% unless frame damage, total-loss, or inoperable.

- condition_grade for vehicles: Good for cosmetic-only; Fair for multiple panels; Poor only for structural/mechanical damage.

Industrial (Generator, Machine):
- Front: control panel or nameplate face.
- Tag: chassis plate or engraved serial plate.
- Heavy rust, oil leaks, missing guards are severe; cosmetic paint chips may be acceptable_wear.
